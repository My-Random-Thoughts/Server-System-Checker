Move Or Copy Resources

Resources can be moved or copied to other groups. This allows you to quickly replicate a configuration from one group to another.

Instructions

To start moving or copying resources around your group structure,

  1. Select the group or sub-group that you want to move/copy resources from,
  2. Select the resources your want to move/copy,
  3. Right-click the selected resources,
  4. Choose   Move/Copy... from the pop-up menu,
  5. From the new window that appears, select the group you want to move/copy the selected resources too
  6. Click OK to perform the move/copy.
