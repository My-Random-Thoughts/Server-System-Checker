Exclude Drives From Scanning

There are times when a server may have specific drive used specifically for a fixed size page-file. In this instance you know the drive in question will always be at 99% full, and therefore always reporting a failure in SSC.

In this window, you can double-click this drive and exclude it from any scanning and reporting functions, allowing you to spend time on the real issues.

Instructions

To exclude a drive from being scanned,

  1. Right-click the server,
  2. Choose   Properties from the pop-up menu,
  3. From the window that appears, click the Exclude Drives button.


    4. From the window that appears, you can see that each drive is listed, along with its current size and available free space.
  4. Double-click the drive you want to exclude from scanning. The drive will move to the "Excluded Drives" group
  5. Click OK to close the Drive Exclusion window.
  6. Click Close to close the Server Properties window.
